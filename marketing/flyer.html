A large language model is fluent but not grounded: it talks about a mortgage or a beneficial owner convincingly, but approximately — and in finance, approximately-right is wrong. Kuber Map gives your agents the precise, shared, auditable language of the business — the industry's standard ontology (FIBO), curated and packaged as grounding context every answer can cite.

Why it's different

Grounding, not a bigger model. The lift is model-robust — a stronger LLM does not close the gap. Every concept carries its FIBO IRI as a citation, and provenance (standard vs. curated) is never blurred, so answers are traceable for audit and regulators.

  • Standards-based — FIBO, the EDM Council's open ontology
  • Audit-ready — citation + provenance on every claim
  • Open & free — MIT, runs in the browser, installable

What's inside

  • 5 curated use cases — loan origination, KYC, securities, regulatory reporting, derivatives
  • Context packs + MCP — drop-in grounding for RAG or an agent, with citations
  • 19 cross-domain bridges — links FIBO doesn't draw, offered back to the EDM Council

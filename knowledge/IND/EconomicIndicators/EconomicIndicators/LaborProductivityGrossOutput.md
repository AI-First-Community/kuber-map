It reflects the change in the input coefficient of labour by industry and can help in the analysis of labour requirements by industry. Shows the time profile of how productively labour is used to generate gross output. Labour productivity changes reflect the joint influence of changes in capital, intermediate inputs, as well as technical, organisational and efficiency change within and between firms, the influence of economies of scale, varying degrees of capacity utilisation and measurement errors."
